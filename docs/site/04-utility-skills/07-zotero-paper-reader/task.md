## Objective

You get a way to read and analyze a paper straight from your Zotero library and pull its citations into a draft: it searches the library by metadata or full text, retrieves and analyzes the PDF section by section, exports BibTeX, and inserts `\cite`/`[@key]` where you want them.

Reach for it whenever you want to work with a paper you already have in Zotero. See [`zotero-paper-reader`](skills/zotero-paper-reader/SKILL.md).
